Stylesheet

The design goal for this website was to create a captivating cosmic experience for the user. I approached the design with a modern, minimalistic mindset, drawing inspiration from the vastness and emptiness of space. This design allows users to engage with the content at their own pace, with ample white space providing moments for reflection and an immersive feeling of browsing the cosmos.

I aimed to incorporate a diverse range of cool and warm colors to enhance the visual appeal of the website and give it a modern touch. These colors not only represent the general feeling of each planet but also add an element of interest without overwhelming the user.

I wanted the font family to give the users a sense of minimalistic futurism, further enhancing the feeling of immersion I wanted to captivate. The use of this font will suit the modern and sleek aesthetic of the website, creating a visually appealing and immersive experience for the users.

The modern decorations create a visual break on the page. The sleek lines guide the user's focus to a central element within the decoration, emphasizing its visual significance.

The banana-based elements on the website add a vibrant and eye-catching visual contrast against the background, enhancing overall visual appeal. These elements also reinforce the modern and playful banana motif of the website.

The sleek and minimalist card design enhances the user experience by creating a sense of immersion in a cosmic space. This design element aligns perfectly with the modern aesthetic of our website.

The navbar follows suit with a modern, sleek design featuring a glass morphic effect that subtly blurs elements behind it. This creates visual interest without being distracting, making it easy for users to locate the navbar when needed.

The solar system data visualization is a display of geometric and mathematical beauty. Its modern and minimalistic design transports the user to a cosmic space as they explore a true-to-scale representation of the solar system.
