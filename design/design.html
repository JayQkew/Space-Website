Style Sheet

The design goal for this website was to create a captivating cosmic experience for the user. I approached the design with a modern, minimalistic mindset, drawing inspiration from the vastness and emptiness of space. This design allows users to engage with the content at their own pace, with ample white space providing moments for reflection and an immersive feeling of browsing the cosmos.
